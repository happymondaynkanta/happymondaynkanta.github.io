Research Collaborators

Prof. Li Jianping

Affiliation: University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC)
Field of Expertise: Wavelet Analysis, Artificial Intelligence, Big Data, Image Processing

Prof. Li Jianping is a distinguished academic leader and director of the International Centre for Wavelet Analysis and Its Applications at UESTC. With over 300 published papers and 30 books, he is internationally known for his pioneering work in wavelet theory, AI, and information security. He has held visiting positions in France, Canada, the U.S., and Switzerland, and has served as general chair of multiple global conferences.

Dr. Ariyo Oluwasanmi

Affiliation: Virgin Media O2, UK
Field of Expertise: Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning

Dr. Ariyo specializes in designing and deploying machine learning pipelines in production environments. His expertise includes MLOps, containerization technologies (Docker, Kubernetes, Kubeflow), and cloud platforms like Google Cloud Platform. His research interests encompass artificial intelligence, machine learning, computer vision, data engineering, and deep learning applications

Dr. Md. Altab Hossin

Affiliation: Chengdu University
Field of Expertise: Specialization: Innovation, Entrepreneurship, AI, and Big Data

Dr. Hossin is a distinguished High-Level Foreign Expert specializing in artificial intelligence, big data, and entrepreneurship. He leads innovation projects and digital labs at Chengdu University. Recognized for his contributions to China-Bangladesh relations, he received the prestigious "Jinsha Friendship Award" in 2024.

Prof. Qi Huang

Affiliation: University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu University of Technology (CDUT)
Field of Expertise: Smart Grids, Power Systems, Wide-Area Measurement, Energy Blockchain, and Big Data

Professor Qi Huang is a renowned expert in electrical engineering, with substantial contributions to smart grid and power system research. He has held key academic roles, including Dean of the School of Energy Science at UESTC, Vice President at CDUT, and a professor at SWUST. A Fellow of IEEE and IET, his research has impacted energy systems globally, earning over 11,000 citations.
