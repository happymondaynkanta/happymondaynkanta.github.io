BiLSTM-Based Inception Multi-Head Attention Network for Predicting Solar Cell Degradation

Abstract

The degradation of solar cells is a critical factor affecting the efficiency and lifespan of photovoltaic (PV) systems. Detecting and diagnosing these degradations early can significantly improve the maintenance and operation of solar farms. This study proposes a novel deep learning framework combining Inception modules, Bi-directional LSTM, and Multi-Head Attention to analyze UAV-captured thermal images of solar panels. The model classifies solar cells into ‚Äúdefected‚Äù and ‚Äúnon-defected‚Äù classes with impressive metrics: 89.65% accuracy, 93.67% precision, and 0.9613 AUC, making it suitable for real-time solar farm monitoring.

Key Contributions

  • Developed a hybrid CNN-BiLSTM-Attention model optimized for thermal image classification of solar cell defects.
  • Achieved robust results across multiple performance metrics (F1-Score: 89.15%, AUC: 0.9613).
  • Designed a user-friendly GUI for uploading and classifying panel images in real-time.
  • Introduced preprocessing techniques that enhance thermal image clarity for model learning.

Conclusion

The proposed IABiLSTM-Net model demonstrates strong potential for practical deployment in smart energy systems. By automating solar panel degradation detection, the system offers a low-cost, scalable, and accurate solution for long-term solar farm maintenance and energy efficiency improvement.

Limitation

Although effective, the model was trained on a relatively small dataset and evaluated on a single environmental condition. Variability in thermal image quality due to weather or camera resolution can affect model generalizability. Real-world deployment may also require hardware integration and calibration.

Future Work

Future improvements will focus on expanding the dataset with multi-weather images, incorporating multispectral data, and extending classification to include degrees of degradation. Integration with drone-based real-time inspection systems is also a promising direction.
