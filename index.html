About Me

About Me

I am a Senior Researcher in the Computing Department of Chengdu University of Technology Oxford Brookes University, Sino-British Collaborative Educational Programme.

I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, working with. My research interest lies broadly in mdecial image processing, deep learning, Bioinformatics, and machine learning. Especially, my recent focus is on applying deep learning to the field of medical image processing (a.k.a, SmartHealth). My projects have involved developing deep learning algorithms for early disease detection and diagnosis to improve public health outcomes. One of my research projects involved developing a deep learning model to detect diabetic retinopathy from fundus scans, which has been widely recognized and cited. My work received the Best Paper Awards (PRAI'21).
