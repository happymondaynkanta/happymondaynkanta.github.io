About Me

About Me

Dr. Happy Nkanta Monday is an Associate Professor at the School of International Education, Chengdu University of Technology Oxford Brookes College.

Dr. Happy Nkanta Monday stands at the forefront of groundbreaking research in the dynamic fields of deep learning, machine vision, machine learning, neural networks, and image processing. As an Associate Professor, he embodies the essence of a Sino-British collaboration in education, driving the boundaries of computational science through innovative research and passionate teaching. Dr. Monday's academic journey is one of exceptional distinction. He holds a master's and a doctoral degree in Electronic Science and Technology and Computer Science and Technology, respectively, both from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). This solid foundation has underpinned a prolific career, with over 75+ academic papers published in journals and conferences, marking him as a leader in his field. Recognition of his contributions includes the prestigious Best Paper Awards (PRAI'21), Best Oral Presentation Awards (ICAIT'2024), and the UESTC Academic and Excellence Performance Award, accolades that highlight his research's impact and his dedication to academic excellence. He is recognized for his expertise in machine learning, deep learning, and computer vision, focusing on applications in medical image processing and analysis. His research, which has garnered over 840 citations with an h-index of 16, emphasizes innovative solutions in AI, wavelet transform, and image recognition. Beyond his achievements, Dr. Monday is a committed educator and innovator, co-founding the computing research team. This initiative not only advances research within the institution but also nurtures the next generation of scientists and engineers. His roles on the undergraduate project committee board further attest to his commitment to academic rigor and the success of his students. In every capacity, whether as a researcher, lecturer, or mentor, Dr. Happy Nkanta Monday exemplifies leadership, dedication, and innovation, contributing significantly to the advancement of computational sciences and shaping the minds that will lead tomorrow's technological frontiers.
