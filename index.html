About Me

About Me

Happy Nkanta Monday is a senior researcher in the computing department of Oxford Brookes University-Chengdu University of Technology, a Sino-British Collaborative Education.

Happy received his Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. His research interest lies broadly in mdecial image processing, deep learning, Bioinformatics, and machine learning. His recent focus is on applying deep learning to the field of medical image processing. His projects involve developing deep learning algorithms for early disease detection and diagnosis to improve public health outcomes. His work received the Best Paper Awards (PRAI'21) and Best Oral Presentation Awards (ICAIT'2024).
